Web Development Page

Web development is the process of creating, maintaining, and managing websites. It is a broad field that encompasses many different aspects, such as:

Front-end development: This is the process of creating the visual elements of a website, such as the layout, design, and interactivity. Front-end developers typically use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Back-end development: This is the process of creating the functionality of a website, such as the database, server-side code, and APIs. Back-end developers typically use programming languages such as PHP, Python, and Java.

Full-stack development: This is the process of developing both the front-end and back-end of a website. Full-stack developers typically have a strong understanding of both HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and programming languages such as PHP, Python, and Java.
